# First-Run Prompt for Students

Students paste this into their assigned OpenClaw on first use.

```text
We are Team XX.

First, help us set up our project identity.

Ask us:
1. Our team name.
2. The name we want to give this OpenClaw agent.
3. Our team members.
4. Our chosen user.
5. The business problem.
6. The current workflow.
7. The AI-native workflow.
8. The human review point.
9. The smallest credible prototype we can build.

Then create or update:
- README.md
- TEAM_LOG.md
- DECISIONS.md
- OPENCLAW_BUILD_LOG.md
- AI_USE_DISCLOSURE.md
- DEPLOYMENT_STATUS.md

Do not build the app yet.
First summarize the plan and ask us to approve the first build step.
```
